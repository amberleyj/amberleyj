Architectural Work

The work below is from a fourth year, second semester project titled: Future Desert Cities. Located in Sun Lakes, Arizona, this project intends to critique suburban sprawl and study its interaction, or lack thereof, with nature. The proposal includes a single-family unit that goes underground in order to create a smaller footprint on the surface of the desert landscape.
