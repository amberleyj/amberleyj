About

I am a houston-born, phoenix-raised creative with a fascination about the relation amongst people, place, design, and nature. Alongside pursuing a degree in architecture, I make and create in a variety of form. This includes digital illustration, music, film, and photography. other interests include repurposing, psychology, storytelling, and sustainability. In short, I am just a creative attempting to attempt, try, and progress.
