Artist Statement

What I choose to create starts with a story. It's all about a narrative and even more about a feeling than anything else. I am fascinated by people, nature, memory, and the perception of space. Softness and simplicity are what I try to exude in order to create relief amongst chaos— something I can only hope people respond to.
